Call for Abstract / Executive Summary and Full Paper

The conference includes workshops, oral/poster presentations and plenary sessions designed to raise the awareness of participants of recent developments in the science and art of cassava, rice and maize agronomy in relation to digital tools. Abstracts of original research papers, demonstration projects, and/or lessons learned from professional experience working with digital technologies are therefore invited.

Presenter eligibility: One of the authors, who is a member of ANA and/or a registered participant of AKILIMO-Lagos-2026 by paying the prescribed Registration Fee, will only be allowed to present the abstract in oral or poster session in-person.
Review process: All submitted abstracts will be transmitted to reviewers and reviewed anonymously for consideration.
Publication: Only abstracts received by the deadline of 31st May 2026 and meeting the prescribed standard will appear in the Book of Abstracts pre-printed before the conference. Only abstracts presented during the conference will be post-printed in the Book of Proceedings.
Submit via Email

Use the online form below for the fastest processing, or email if you have a full paper attachment.

Guide for Abstract / Executive Summary Preparation
  • Not more than 300 words
  • Written in Times New Roman, 12pt, single spaced
  • Must state briefly: objective(s), methodology, major findings and conclusion(s)
  • No citations, tables, figures or illustrations in the abstract body
  • Must include: Title (centered), list of author(s) and affiliations
  • Identify corresponding author's email address and phone number (preferably WhatsApp)

Full Paper Requirements
Maximum 20 A4 pages (1 inch margins all sides), including tables, figures and illustrations
  • 1
    Title — centered, bold, concise. Not exceeding 21 words
  • 2
    Title Page — Authors, Affiliations, Corresponding author's email & phone
  • 3
    Abstract — ≤ 300 words
  • 4
    Keywords — up to 6
  • 5
    Introduction — background, problem statement, justification and objective
  • 6
    Materials & Methods — study design, procedures and analysis clearly described for reproducibility
  • 7
    Results — factual; tables/figures with legends and numbered in text
  • 8
    Discussion & Conclusion — interpret findings, compare to literature, limitations, future direction
  • 9
    Declaration — of Interests, Funding & confirmation of no AI-assisted writing
  • 10
    References — formatted as per APA reference style
Submission Process — Step by Step
  • 1
    Submit your abstract first using this online form or via email by 31st May 2026
  • 2
    Receive acceptance notification by 30th June 2026
  • 3
    Complete your conference registration (ANA membership or registration fee required to present)
  • 4
    Submit your full paper by 31st July 2026 via technicalpaper@akilimonigeria.org
  • 5
    Present your work at the conference in-person (oral or poster session)
