Training & Capacity Building

Comprehensive training programs for farmers and value chain actors

Farmer Training

Hands-on training for smallholder farmers on modern cassava production techniques, AKILIMO tool usage, and best agricultural practices.

Extension Officer Training

Training programs for agricultural extension officers to effectively use and promote AKILIMO tools within their communities.

Value Chain Training

Specialized training for value chain actors including processors, aggregators, and marketers to optimize the entire cassava value chain.

Digital Literacy

Training programs to improve digital literacy among farmers, enabling them to effectively use mobile and web-based agricultural tools.

Sustainable Practices

Training on sustainable and climate-smart agricultural practices to ensure long-term environmental and economic benefits.

Market Linkages

Training on market analysis, price negotiation, and connecting farmers with reliable buyers and financial services.
