Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about AKILIMO

You can access AKILIMO tools by registering on our platform. Click "Get Started" or "Access AKILIMO Tools" to create your account and begin receiving site-specific agricultural recommendations.

We offer comprehensive training programs for farmers, extension officers, and value chain actors. Our programs cover AKILIMO tool usage, sustainable farming practices, digital literacy, and market linkages.

We welcome partnerships with organizations committed to agricultural transformation. Contact us through this form or email us directly to discuss partnership opportunities and collaboration models.

Basic AKILIMO tools are free for farmers. We also offer advanced features and services for partner organizations and commercial clients. Contact us for detailed pricing information.
