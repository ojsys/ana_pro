General Questions

AKILIMO is a digital platform that provides site-specific agricultural recommendations to help farmers optimize cassava production and increase profits. The platform uses data analytics to provide personalized advice on planting, fertilizer use, and farming practices.

You can access AKILIMO tools by registering on our platform. Simply click "Get Started" or "Access AKILIMO Tools" to create your account and begin receiving site-specific recommendations for your farm.

Basic AKILIMO tools are free for smallholder farmers. We also offer advanced features and premium services for partner organizations and commercial clients. Contact us for more information about pricing.
